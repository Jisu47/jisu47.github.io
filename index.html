Profile summary

What changed from the previous version

The old page was a single centered hero block. This redesign moves your identity and links into a permanent sidebar, then turns the rest of the page into readable sections with a more durable information architecture.

Why this structure works

It stays simple enough for GitHub Pages, but it feels closer to a portfolio or lab homepage. You can keep adding cards, notes, and links without redesigning the whole site every time.

How this site can grow

Profile

Identity first

The sidebar keeps your name, links, and page structure visible at all times, which makes the site feel grounded even before you add a large amount of content.

Archive

Section-based growth

You can keep extending the page by adding project cards, note cards, reading logs, or release updates without changing the overall design language.

Delivery

Low maintenance

The implementation stays lightweight: one HTML file, a small amount of JavaScript, and no build step. That makes it easy to keep publishing from GitHub.
